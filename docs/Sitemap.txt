Home Page:
    - Submit IT Support Ticket (Button)
        - When users click on this button, they will be brought to the Submit Ticket Page
    - Employee Login (Button) 
        - When users click on this button, they will be brought to the Employee Login Page

Submit Ticket Page:
    - Submit Ticket (Form)
        - Users can fill out this form to create a new IT Support Ticket that employees can work on
        - Users will need to provide the following information:
            1) A short/descriptive
            2) The type of help needed
            3) The urgency of the help needed
            4) A description of the support needed
            5) The client's full name
            6) The client's email address
        - Once a user submits the ticket form, they will be notified and brought back to the Home Page

Employee Login Page:
    - Employee Login (Form):
        - Employees can provide their email and matching password to login to their employee acount
        - Once an employee submits the login form, they will be brought to their Employee Dashboard Page

Employee Dashboard Page:
    - Current Tickets (Module)
        - This module will include a list of all of the tickets this specific employee is currently working on
        - These tickets will be ordered by their urgency first, then by the date they were submitted (earliest towards the top)
        - Each individual ticket will be inclosed in a Ticket Card
    - Ticket (Card 1)
        - This card will display a brief overview of that specific ticket (title, type, urgency, status and date submitted)
        - When an employee clicks on this card, a Ticket Modal will pop up with more information about the Ticket with more available actions
    - Ticket (Modal 1)
        - This modal will display ALL of the information of that specific ticket (titile, type, urgency, description, client info, date submitted and the current status of the ticket).
        - This modal will include a <Select> box to where an employee can change the current status of the ticket (Backlog, Pending, Doing, Complete)
        - This modal will also include a Ticket Contact Form to where the employee can send a quick message via email to the client of this specific ticket
        - This modal will also include a button that an employee can click if they wish to delete this specific ticket
    - Ticket Contact (Form)
        - This form will include just a simple multi-line text box to where an employee can type a message to send to the client
        - Once an employee submits the contact form, an email will be sent to the client with their given email

Employee Profile Page:
    - Personal Info (Card)
        - This card will include all of the information of this specific employee (Email, Password, First and Last Name)
        - This card will include a Button that when an employee clicks on it, the Edit Info Modal will pop up
    - Edit Info (Modal Form)
        - This modal form will include a form where an employee can update all of their given information (email, password, first and last name)
        - These text boxes will be prefilled with the information that already exists
        - Once an employee submits this form, they will be brought back to their Profile Page with the update information
    - Completed Tickets (Module)
        - This module will include a list of all of the tickets this specific employee has already completed
        - These tickets will be ordered by the date that they were completed (earliest towards the top)
        - Each individual ticket will be inclosed in a Ticket Card (mentioned in the Employee Dashboard Page Section)

Ticket Board Page:
    - All Tickets (Module)
        - This module will include all of the tickets that are currently not being worked on by other employees
        - These tickets will be ordered by their urgency first, then by the date they were submitted (earliest towards the top)
        - Each individual ticket will be inclosed in a Ticket Card (mentioned in the Employee Dashboard Page Section)
    - Ticket (Card 2)
        - This card will display a brief overview of that specific ticket (title, type, urgency, status and date submitted)
        - When an employee clicks on this card, a Ticket Modal will pop up with more information about the Ticket with more available actions
    - Ticket (Modal 2)
        - This modal will display ALL of the information of that specific ticket (titile, type, urgency, description, client info, date submitted and the current status of the ticket).
        - This modal will also include a button that an employee can click if they wish to add it to their Current Tickets Module on their Employee Dashboard Page
        - This modal will also include a button that an employee can click if they wish to delete this specific ticket
